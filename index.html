Our Mission

Maverick's Mission is a non-profit organization that rescues and cares for farmed and companion animals who were abandoned, abused, or neglected. We at Maverick's Mission encourage more humane and compassionate behaviors and believe that each living creature has value and deserves to live free of suffering and exploitation.

Who We Are

We are Maverick, the pig, and Jennifer, the human.   We first met in 2015 when I, Maverick, was just a little piglet. I was in need of a home and my soon-to-be new mom decided to adopt me.

Little did she know that my seven pound self would spark an enduring love and passion within her, or that over the next seven years, she would slowly become home to over seventy different pigs, goats, and dogs needing a place to call home. Maverick's Mission, named after the one and only (me), was officially started in 2019 as a 501c animal sanctuary.

Although there have been some big changes around here, what has remained the same is my mom's and Maverick's Mission's dedication to providing a quality life and care to animals who are in need of a forever home.
