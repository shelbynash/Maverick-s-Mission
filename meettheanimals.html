Maverick's Mission rescues farmed animals that are often victims of cruelty and neglect. Many of the residents have escaped slaughterhouses, have been rescued from closing farms and auctions, or are surrendered to us. When we say yes to rescuing an animal, we say yes to providing them love and care for the rest of their life.
